Use your phone or computer's camera to scan the unique Royal Mail barcode in the home test kit.

This appears on the right-hand side of the label. Do not scan the QR code, which is on the left.

The Royal Mail barcode is also different from the home test kit barcode.

You can also manually enter the 13 character reference below the barcode.
